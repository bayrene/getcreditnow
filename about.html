About GetCreditNow

Helping Americans understand their options for getting out of debt.

Our Mission

GetCreditNow.com exists for one reason: to help people who are struggling with debt find clear, honest information about their options.

We know that being in debt is stressful. It affects your sleep, your relationships, and your sense of control over your own life. And when you go looking for help online, you're met with a confusing mix of sales pitches, conflicting advice, and jargon-heavy articles that leave you more confused than when you started.

We believe you deserve better than that.

What We Do

GetCreditNow.com is an independent financial education resource. We research, write, and publish free guides on topics like:

  • How debt relief and debt settlement actually work
  • How to improve your credit score
  • How to compare your options — consolidation, settlement, counseling, and more
  • What questions to ask and what red flags to watch for

We also connect readers with reputable debt relief services when appropriate. We've partnered with National Debt Relief — a BBB A+ accredited company that has helped over 100,000 Americans resolve their debt — because we believe they offer a legitimate, helpful service for people with significant unsecured debt.

Our Editorial Standards

We hold ourselves to high standards because financial content has real consequences for real people:

  • Honesty over hype: We include the pros AND cons of every option we discuss. Debt settlement affects your credit score — we tell you that. Bankruptcy has serious consequences — we explain them. You can't make good decisions with half the information.
  • Education over sales: Our primary goal is to help you understand your options, not to pressure you into any particular service. If debt settlement isn't right for your situation, we'll say so.
  • Transparency about money: We earn revenue through affiliate partnerships — meaning we receive a commission when readers click through to our partners and take action. This comes at no additional cost to you. We disclose this clearly on every page. See our full affiliate disclosure.
  • Accuracy: We base our content on data from authoritative sources including the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau (CFPB), the Federal Trade Commission (FTC), the Federal Reserve, and established financial research.

What We Are Not

To be clear about what GetCreditNow.com is not:

  • We are not a financial advisor. Our content is educational and should not be taken as personalized financial advice.
  • We are not a credit repair company. We don't repair credit, negotiate debts, or provide any financial services directly.
  • We are not a lender. We don't offer loans, credit cards, or any financial products.

We're an information resource that helps you understand your options and connects you with reputable services when appropriate. For personalized financial advice, we always recommend consulting with a qualified financial professional.
